Mastoiditis: Causes, Symptoms, and Advanced ENT Care in Kovilpatti

Mastoiditis is a serious infection of the mastoid bone, located just behind the ear. At Nizmed ENT Clinic in Kovilpatti, our ENT specialists provide expert diagnosis and advanced treatment for mastoiditis, ensuring the best outcomes for patients in Kovilpatti, Thoothukudi, Kayathar, Ettayapuram, and surrounding regions.

What is Mastoiditis? Mastoiditis occurs when a middle ear infection spreads to the mastoid bone, leading to swelling, pain, and sometimes hearing loss. Early detection and treatment are crucial to prevent complications.

"If you or your child experience persistent ear pain, swelling behind the ear, or discharge, seek immediate ENT consultation at Nizmed ENT Clinic, Kovilpatti."

Common Symptoms of Mastoiditis

  • Severe ear pain and tenderness behind the ear
  • Swelling or redness around the ear
  • Fever and irritability (especially in children)
  • Hearing loss or ear discharge
  • Headache and general malaise
